I did not want the music part of the site to be an afterthought hidden in bookmarks or playlist screenshots.

A small dedicated page is enough:

- it gives me a place to keep a hand-picked list
- it lets me add context when a song matters for a specific reason
- it keeps the site personal instead of purely professional
