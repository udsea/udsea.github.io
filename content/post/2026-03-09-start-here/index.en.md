This site has three lanes:

- technical write-ups when I want to explain something properly
- shorter notes on day-to-day affairs when an idea is still fresh
- a music page for songs that survive repeated listening

The homepage stays simple on purpose. This pinned post acts as the map, and the rest of the page stays reserved for the latest writing.

## What To Expect

The technical posts will be slower and more detailed.

The day-to-day posts will be lighter, faster, and closer to a notebook.

The music section is where I keep a list of tracks worth returning to, not a complete listening history.
